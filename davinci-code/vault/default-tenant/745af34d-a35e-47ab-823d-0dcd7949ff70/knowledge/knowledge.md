# VentureVilla - Knowledge Base

## Overview

VentureVilla is a startup center located in a historic villa in Hannover, Germany, near the Eilenriede park. It provides early-stage startups with the resources and support they need to develop their business ideas. VentureVilla emphasizes strong, long-term networking among founders, alumni, and partners. Its core offering is a 100-day accelerator program, held twice a year, that provides startups with individual coaching, targeted mentoring, and access to a network of investors and experts. Additionally, VentureVilla offers fixed offices and flexible workspaces to facilitate focused work and collaboration. Alumni often remain connected with VentureVilla, acting as mentors, tenants, or event participants, fostering a vibrant community that supports and learns from each other.

VentureVilla's mission is to create a supportive ecosystem where early-stage startups can thrive. They achieve this by providing not only physical space and resources but also a strong emphasis on community and networking. This approach helps startups gain access to valuable expertise, potential investors, and a supportive peer group. VentureVilla's value proposition lies in its ability to accelerate the growth of early-stage startups through its comprehensive program and vibrant community, positioning it as a central hub for innovation and entrepreneurship in the Hannover region. VentureVilla is like a large start-up shared apartment, where you can find exactly the environment for success, performance and balance that every founder needs.
