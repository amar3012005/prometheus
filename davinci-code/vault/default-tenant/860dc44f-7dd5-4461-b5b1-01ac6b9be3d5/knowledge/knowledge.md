## Pricing (if available)

Femona Luxury Properties offers a variety of pricing options to meet the needs of our clients:

*   **Sales Commissions:** Standard real estate commissions apply for sales transactions, typically ranging from 5% to 6% of the sale price. This commission is split between the seller's agent and the buyer's agent. Specific commission rates are negotiable and will be agreed upon in writing prior to listing a property for sale.
*   **Rental Commissions:** Rental commissions vary depending on the length of the lease and local market conditions. Typically, landlords pay a commission equal to one month's rent for securing a tenant. In some cases, tenants may be responsible for paying the commission.
*   **Property Management Fees:** Property management fees are typically a percentage of the monthly rental income, ranging from 8% to 12%. This fee covers all aspects of property management, including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance, and repairs.
*   **Consulting Fees:** Consulting fees for investment advice and other specialized services are determined on a case-by-case basis, depending on the scope of the engagement. We provide detailed fee proposals outlining the scope of work and associated costs.
*   **New Development Marketing Fees:** Fees for marketing new luxury developments are negotiated with developers and are typically based on a percentage of the total sales revenue of the project.
*   **Concierge Service Fees:** Fees for concierge services, such as relocation assistance and interior design consultations, are determined on a case-by-case basis, depending on the scope of the services provided.
*   **Retainer Fees:** For clients requiring ongoing services and dedicated support, we offer retainer agreements with monthly or annual fees. The retainer fee covers a set number of hours of service and provides priority access to our team.
*   **Custom Pricing Plans:** We are happy to create custom pricing plans to meet the specific needs of our clients. Please contact us to discuss your requirements and receive a personalized quote.

**Note:** All pricing is subject to change and is based on market conditions. A detailed agreement will be provided prior to any services being rendered.
